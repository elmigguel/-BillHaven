### 3.2 StepN (Move-to-Earn Pioneer)

**Peak Stats:**
- 700K+ monthly active users (peak)
- Current: 35K MAU (2024) - still largest M2E by market cap
- Built on Solana blockchain

**Dual-Token Economy:**

**GST (Green Satoshi Token):**
- Unlimited supply (inflationary)
- Earned through physical activity
- Used for in-game upgrades, breeding, repairs
- Burn mechanisms to control inflation

**GMT (Green Metaverse Token):**
- Fixed supply: 6 billion
- Governance token
- Deflationary (halves every 3 years)
- 30% distributed via M2E and governance

**Game Mechanics:**

**NFT Sneakers:**
- 4 types: Walking, Jogging, Running, Trainer
- 4 attributes: Efficiency, Luck, Comfort, Resilience
- Higher Efficiency = more GST per Energy spent
- Purchase required to start earning

**Energy System:**
- Limits earning time per session
- 1 Common sneaker = 2 Energy = 10 minutes earning
- More/better sneakers = more Energy = more earning time
- Prevents 24/7 exploitation

**Durability System:**
- Sneakers degrade with use
- Requires GST to repair
- Higher Resilience = slower degradation
- Creates GST sink (deflationary pressure)

**Breeding System:**
- Combine 2 sneakers to create new NFT
- Costs GST + AXS tokens
- New sneaker has unique traits
- Creates new NFT to sell or use

**Retention Challenges (Why 95% Drop in Users):**
- Tokenomics not sustainable long-term
- High barrier to entry (expensive NFT sneakers)
- GST inflation outpaced new user growth
- Earning > Spending = token death spiral
- Need constant new users to replace cashing out players

**2024 Recovery Efforts:**
- Adidas collaboration (1,200 co-branded physical shoes)
- FSL ID launch + 100M GMT airdrop
- Improved tokenomics
- Bridging digital/physical experiences

**BillHaven Application:**

**What to Adopt:**
1. Dual-token economy (BLC utility + governance)
2. NFT profile pictures/badges (tradeable achievements)
3. Energy system (daily trading limits for new users, increases with trust)
4. Durability/maintenance (pay small BLC to maintain benefits)
5. Breeding/crafting (combine badges to create rare badges)

**What to Avoid:**
1. High barrier to entry (NO expensive NFTs required to start)
2. Unsustainable tokenomics (ensure rewards < fees generated)
3. Ponzi dynamics (need real value creation, not just new user money)
4. Over-complexity (StepN is confusing for newcomers)

**Specific BillHaven Features:**
- Free to start, earn BLC tokens from trading
- BLC tokens unlock premium features
- Stake BLC to reduce trading fees (burn mechanism)
- Limited daily earning for new users (anti-exploitation)
- Earning increases with trust level (incentive to build reputation)
